About Us

The Rabeya's Foundation is a non-profit, non-government and charitable organization that was established in 2024. The organization has been actively engaged to facilitate humanitarian and social development. Rabeya's Foundation is committed to designing and implementing pragmatic programs to develop resources, putting the best alternative uses of assets and opportunities.

Our mission is to improve the lives of those in need.
